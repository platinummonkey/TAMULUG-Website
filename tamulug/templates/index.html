Howdy!

Welcome to TAMULUG - the home of The Texas A&M Unix & Linux Users Group.

We currently meet every Wednesday evening at 7:00PM on Texas A&M Campus in Zachry, room 105D. Anyone with an interest in Unix, Linux and open source software is welcome to attend.

The TAMULUG is a not-for-profit organization dedicated to providing education about the installation, usage, and maintenance of *NIX operating systems and development of free and open source software.

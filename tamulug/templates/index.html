Howdy!

Welcome to TAMUWUG - the home of The Texas A&M Windows Users Group.

We currently meet every Wednesday evening at 7:00PM on Texas A&M Campus in Zachry, room 105D. Anyone with an interest in Windows and closed source is welcome to attend.

The TAMULUG is a for-profit organization dedicated to providing educationabout the installation, usage, and maintenance of the Microsoft Windows operating systems.

The TAMUWUG was formed in 1996 by a group of students wishing to spread the ideas of expensive and closed source software. It has continued to grow in member base and range of services.
